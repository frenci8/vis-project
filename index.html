Brief description
The prototype below shows via a TreeMap where the 2017 budget of the U.S. Department of Education, Training, Employment, and Social Services went. The budget was $174.6 Billion.
Visualization prototype
Manual
•
Click on one of the boxes to go the next level of budget function.
Current levels are:
Budget function -> Budget sub-function -> Federal account -> Program activity


•
In order to go to the previous level, click on the blue navigator which is located on top of the prototype.
